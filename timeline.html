A Brief History of the Ethics Bowl China

Click on each block to read full news!

2019
5 Sep

The Shanghai Interscholastic Ethics Bowl (SIEB) was officially founded. We established contact with the US Ethics Bowl and published our first website.

1 Oct

We have a team of eight in charge of all logistics of the SIEB program. We reached out to schools and institutions to explore opportunities for collaboration in recruiting judges and teams. Meanwhile, we started to develop the official documents of our Ethics Bowl competition, adapting from the original US Ethics Bowl documents.

25 Dec

Having just updated our website to a brand new version, the SIEB is officially open for registration. We made our event totally free of charge for students and that even includes our online courses!

2020
9 Jan

We have overall 324 students (64 teams) from 15 schools across 2 provinces who registered for our event!
13 Jan

More than 180 students joined our live information sessions.
16 Jan

We conducted interviews for students based on our self-designed rubric. 12 teams were selected to participate in the competition, though all candidates can still join our online seminars.
22 Jan

The SIEB was postponed due to the COVID-19 outbreak. We strived to find alternative solutions for our event.
7 Feb

Since the pandemic, we moved our ethical discussions online. On Feb 7th, we invited the US Ethics Bowl director and coaches to speak at one of our online seminars. They shared with our participants some valuable advice on preparing for the Ethics Bowl competition.
19 Feb

We launched our first-ever Ethics Bowl case competition! High schoolers nationwide can submit their own case that reflects their thoughts on the news they've learned during the pandemic.
22-25 Feb

Another three online seminars with participants and coaches are held. Led by our instructors, students discussed the ethical cases in breakout rooms after each lecture. Teams will submit the assignments to be graded and commented by our instructors.

2 April

We launched the first Australian/Chinese Ethics Bowl Friendly in collaboration with the Australasian Ethics Olympiad.
28 April

The Ethics Bowl China (EBC) was founded after we made plans to develop affiliated regional Ethics Bowls across China.
22 June

We have our first affiliated regional competition: the Sichuan Chapter of the Ethics Bowl China! Click here to visit their webiste. The EBC provides all kinds of support for our regional organizers, including promotions, fundraising, materials, and international collaboration opportunities with our partners.

25 June

In collaboration with the Australasian Ethics Olympiad and the Canadian Ethics Olympiad, we launched the first formal International Ethics Bowl/Olympiad, with 6 teams from three different countries and judges from 9 different time zones across the world.
9 July

The EBC director Leo Huang joined an online meeting with the US, UK, Australia, and Canada Ethics Bowl/Olympiad directors. We all agreed to explore opportunities for more global collaborations in the future.

2 Aug

The Shanghai Interscholastic Ethics Bowl Online Competition is launched. Although not having the chance to communicate face-to-face, participants still enjoyed a whole day of ethical discussions with each other.
