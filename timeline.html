A Brief History of the Ethics Bowl China

2019
5 Sep

The Shanghai Interscholastic Ethics Bowl (SIEB) was officially founded. We established contact with the US Ethics Bowl and published our first website. Read More

1 Oct

We now have a team of 8 in charge of all logistics of the SIEB program. We reached out to schools and institutions to explore opportunities for collaboration in recruiting judges and teams. Meanwhile, we started to develop the official documents of our Ethics Bowl competition, adapting from the original US Ethics Bowl versions.

25 Dec

The SIEB is now officially open for registration. We made our event totally free of charge for students and that even includes our online courses! Read More

9 Jan

Registration is closed. We have overall 324 students (64 teams) from 15 schools across 2 provinces who registered for our event! Read More

2014
14 Jul

Travels to France, Italy, Spain, and Peru. After completing fashion editorial in Lima, prolongs stay to make portraits of local people in a daylight studio

2016
28 Aug

Upon moving to Brooklyn that summer, I began photographing weddings in Chicago
